OnigiNoy, a rice ball businesscreated by 22 students during their Entrepreneurship course,specializesin unique viand-infused rice balls. Ourinspiration comesfrom the Japanese 'Onigiri,'whichwe've adapted to 'OnigiNoy,' meaning 'produced by Pinoy'—a nod to our Filipino roots. The originalconcept,conceived by one of ourstudents,transformsthis traditional productinto a rice ballfilledwith Filipino viand, using rice as our primary ingredient.
Our Goalsisto cultivate cultural appreciation through our Filipinoinspired products, delivering unforgettable flavorsthatcapture the essence oflocalside dishes. We strive to be the preferred choice forthose seeking an affordable, on-the-go rice mealwith a taste of perfection.
Our packaging is both reusable and food-safe for delivery. With a totalcapital of1610 pesoscontributed by our 22 student members,we operate from Kay Talişe St.,Dr. A. Santos Ave., SanDionisio, Parañaque City,wherewe produce, package,store, and sell our products.
Beyond being a delicious meal, ourrice balls offer a unique blend ofrice and variousside dishes, appealing to a diverse customer base, including students and individuals of all ages. We provide an irresistible taste at affordable prices. To expand ourreach,OnigiNoy employs a strategic approach, leveraging social media promotion and pre-order availability to establish a distinctive presence in the food industry.
